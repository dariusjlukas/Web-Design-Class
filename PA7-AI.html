Academics

Olin practices an unconventional teaching method, and thus the classrooms are designed to be equally unconventional. One thing you will not find at Olin is a traditional lecture hall, save the one in the admissions building, but this is mostly used for presenting to guests, or for guest speakers. The majority of classrooms are small, and designed to encourage collaboration between students. One core value at Olin is teamwork, and this is reflected in the many team projects and activities.

Another important aspect of Olin is the unique way it treats students. At Olin, students are considered partners in the business, and expected to help the college achieve its mission. Because of this, Olin is highly selective about who they admit, and puts a lot of attention into each student.
