Why does Olin Exist?

Olin College of Engineering is an elite engineering-only school dedicated to educating in a hands-on way. They accomplish this through hands-on projects and a unique style of learning.

The Olin Mission

Olin seeks to reinvent the way engineering is taught. As we like to say, Olin has never known a time without google. This speaks volumes about how modern Olin is, and how old traditional ways of teaching engineering are. Olin seeks to improve this through collaboration with other Universities through the colaboratory, and be producing some of the best tech graduates around.
